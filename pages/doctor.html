Dr. Arun Dhawale, MBBS, MS, MCh, is a Board Certified Plastic, Reconstructive & Cosmetic surgeon who has rapidly become one of the most sought after aesthetic surgery practitioners in India. She led the department of Plastic Surgery at Aster CMI Hospital, Bangalore, solo from 2016 -2018. She is also associated with Apollo Spectra Hospital, and Cytecare Cancer Hospital in Bangalore. Sometimes a rare combination of qualities and passion come together to produce unique talent.
Dr. Arun Dhawale's skill and passion for cosmetic and aesthetic surgery draws patients from across India and abroad. Often, no amount of dieting and gymming can get rid of stubborn fat in certain areas of the body, for example the waistline, the abdomen and thighs.
