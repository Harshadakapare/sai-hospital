Transforming health care

Sai MultispecialityHospital is the largest integrated, not-for-profit medical group practice in the world. We're building the future, one where the best possible care is available to everyone — and more people can heal at home. Our relentless research turns into earlier diagnoses and new cures. That's how we inspire hope in those who need it most.

At Sai Multispeciality Hospital, experts work together to solve the most challenging unmet needs of patients. Our history of innovation dates back almost 150 years, when brothers Will an integrated, team-based approach to medicine. Today, that trailblazing spirit drives innovations like sai Hospital — which powers new technologies to change how care is delivered to all.
